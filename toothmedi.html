—MEDICAL REMIDY —

1.AMOXYWYN 500mg CAPSULE

Amoxywyn 500mg capsule is a penicillin type of antibiotic which is used for treating wide variety of bacterial infections of acute otitis media, sinusitis, lung infections, cystitis, gonorrhoea, dental abscess, typhoid, tonsilitis, endocarditis (where the inner walls of heart get swollen), pyelonephritis. It is also effective in treating pneumonia and peptic ulcers by eliminating a bacterium known as Helicobacter pylori. This medicine is not advised for treating a common cold, flu or other viral infections since it is not effective in treating these infections.


MORNING(1)---AFTERNOON(1)-----NIGHT(1) AFTER LUNCH 3 DAYS.
2.FLAGYL 400mg

Uses of Flagyl Tablet Flagyl 400 Tablet is used in the treatment of bacterial infections and parasitic infections. It is used in infections of the brain, reproductive system, gastrointestinal tract, skin, vagina, and other areas of the body. Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Swallow it as a whole.

3.ZERODOL SP

Uses of Zerodol-SP Tablet Zerodol-SP Tablet is used for pain relief. It relieves pain in conditions like headache, mild migraine, musculoskeletal pain or painful menses. Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Swallow it as a whole.



MORNING(1)---AFTERNOON(1)-----NIGHT(1) AFTER LUNCH 3 DAYS.
4.RABWYN DSR

Rabewin-DSR Capsule is a combination medicine used to treat gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux) and peptic ulcer disease by relieving the symptoms of acidity such as heartburn, stomach pain, or irritation. It also neutralizes the acid and promotes easy passage of gas to reduce stomach discomfort. Rabewin-DSR Capsule is taken without food in a dose and duration as advised by the doctor.
