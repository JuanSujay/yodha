What are the sympotoms for eye issues and what are its home and medical remides

SYMPOTOMS IF EYE ISSUES:- Eye infections can be uncomfortable and even painful. Certain conditions, if left untreated it can become serious. Some common eye conditions are:-


-Pink eye also known as conjunctivitis
-Dry eye which occurs when you tear ducts can't lubricant the eye properly
-keratitis an infection of cornea
When To Visit Doctor:-
-Pain or Tenderness
-Discharge
-Persistently red eyes
-Senstivity to light
If your child or baby shows any signs of having eye infection take them straight to a doctor .
