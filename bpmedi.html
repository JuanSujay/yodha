1.ERITEL 40

Eritel 40 Tablet is a medicine used to treat high blood pressure and heart failure. Lowering blood pressure helps to prevent future heart attacks and strokes. This medicine is also effective in preserving kidney function in patients with diabetes. Eritel 40 Tablet can be prescribed either alone or in combination with other medicines.


Take this Tablet after food.
